Richard Branson's Noteable Achievement Timeline

Given Richard's sizeable accomplishments and the many brands of the Virgin Group this timeline will focus on the early growth of Richard and his Virgin brand. His space travel company, Virgin Galactic was announced in 2012.

  • 1968 First issue of "Student" Richard's magazine and first venture.
  • 1970 Richard starts selling mail order records under the name Virgin,this was in part to support his magazine.
  • 1971 First Virgin Records Storefront opens.
  • 1973 Virgin Record Label Begins. Virgin eventually becomes one of the top 6 record companies in the world.
  • 1977 Virgin signs the Sex Pistols.
  • 1978 Richard Branson buys Necker in the British Virgin Islands in order to impress his future wife Joan Templeton. At the the time the island sold for 180,000 GBP.
  • 1979 The first Virgin Megastore opens in London, until its closing in 2007.
  • 1979 Virgin Films launches with its first title "The Great Rock n'Roll Swindle", featuring the Sex Pistols.
  • 1979 Virgin Books established to publish music titles to compliment the Virgin Records label.
  • 1980 Virgin Records goes global.
  • 1983 Virgin Games computer game publishing started.
  • Virigin Vision launches "Music Box" a 24 hour music radio station.
  • 1984 Virgin Atlantic Airways and Air Cargo founded.
  • 1985 Virgin Holidays travel agency introduced.
  • 1987 Virgin Records come to the United States and Japan.
  • 1987 The Virgin Balloon and Airship comapany are launched.
  • 1988 The first version of Virgin Hotels opens in the UK.
  • 1988 Virgin Classics, a record label featuring classical music opens.
  • 1991 Virgin Publishing is founded and will eventually become Virgin Books.
  • 1992 in an attempt to save his airline business, Richard sells Virgin Records to Thorn EMI. He later states that this was one the hardest saddest decisions he has made in life.
  • 1993 Virgin Radio goes live across the UK.
